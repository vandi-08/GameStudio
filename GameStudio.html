Story

Wuthering Waves: In a post-apocalyptic world, humanity strives to rebuild after a catastrophic event called the "Lament." You play as Rover, awakening without memories, exploring a dynamic open world filled with mysteries, monsters, and ancient secrets.

Black Myth: Wukong: Inspired by the Chinese epic "Journey to the West," you play as the Destined One, a monkey warrior fighting through legendary foes and discovering ancient lore. The game offers a rich mythology-based narrative full of action and magic.
